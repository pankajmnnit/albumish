New in beta

Find photos by face, locally.

Choose one clear reference photo and Albumish scans your folder for likely matches. It only adds likely matching photos to that review, so you can quickly find one person across a large wedding or event album.

Why it feels fast

Designed to avoid decision fatigue.

Albumish reduces repeated decisions by grouping similar shots, saving progress automatically, and letting you stop early when your shortlist is good enough.

  • Groups similar shots into smaller batches.
  • Finds likely face matches with local AI beta.
  • Lets you inspect a photo larger before selecting it.
  • Supports selecting one or multiple photos from a group.
  • Saves dark or blurry photos for later review.
  • Lets you pause, resume, or export selected photos early.
  • Copies selected photos without modifying originals.
